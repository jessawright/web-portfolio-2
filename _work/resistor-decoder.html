Goal

Make a single page app to decode the colored bands on through-hole resistors and display their value.

Development

  • I used this project primarily as a way to learn React.
  • I learned a lot about SVG: making vector drawings in Inkscape/Illustrator, optimization methods and considerations, implementations methods, and syntax.
  • Along the way, I also learned Sass and Webpack.

Design

  • I designed the entire UI to look like components on a printed circuit board: a resistor, lit and unlit LEDs for radio buttons, and LCDs to display the resistor value.
  • I chose all the colors by taking color samples from photos of printed circuit boards, resistors, LCDs, and LEDs.
  • The resistor shape began as a vector tracing of a photo of a resistor, then I cleaned it up and made it symmetrical.
  • I chose fonts based on what is typically seen on circuit boards and LCDs.

Tools

  • HTML5/CSS3
  • Sass/SCSS
  • Javascript
  • React
  • SVG
