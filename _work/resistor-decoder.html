Project

Make a single page app to decode the colored bands on through-hole resistors and display their value.

Development

  • I used this project primarily as a way to learn React, SVG, Sass, and Webpack while making something interesting.
  • After sketching a general UI plan, I drew a flowchart of React components and the information flow between them.
  • After making a resistor SVG, I broke up the SVG code to assign to several different React components. Once I had some basic functions and information flow in place, I created a JSON to store all the information necessary to actually decode resistors, then added those decoding functions.

Design

  • I designed the entire UI to look like components on a printed circuit board: a resistor, LEDs for radio buttons, and LCDs to display the resistor value.
  • I chose all the colors by taking color samples from photos of printed circuit boards, resistors, LCDs, and LEDs.
  • The resistor shape began as a vector tracing of a photo of a resistor, then I cleaned it up and made it symmetrical.
  • I chose fonts based on what is typically seen on circuit boards and LCDs.
